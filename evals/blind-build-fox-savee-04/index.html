why it works

It borrows the seriousness of a relic and the tempo of a club tool.

From Relic, the page takes the layered chamber logic: title as architecture, copy as atmosphere, object as evidence. From Jazz Keys, it borrows the single-stage confidence and direct-response rhythm. The result is a release page that feels authored, not assembled.

The bright signal accent comes from the playfulness of Design your own hole, which keeps the drop from collapsing into tasteful darkness.
