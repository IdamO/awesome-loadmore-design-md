# Reference Selection

I navigated the repo the way the agent guide intended:

1. `AGENTS.md` for the retrieval strategy.
2. `data/agent-index.json` plus the collection docs in `collections/`.
3. Specific `design-md/*/README.md` and screenshots to verify the visual tone before recombining.

## Picked references

- `design-md/hybrid-vigor` — the base world. I used its white-field abstract blobs, color tension, and net.art confidence as the core atmosphere for the page.
- `design-md/amanda-yeah-piano-scrollbar-piano` — the rhythm layer. I pulled its soft gradient drift and playful motion language so the page can feel alive without becoming dashboardy.
- `design-md/developments` — the editorial spine. I used its oversized typography and clean, poster-like structure to keep the landing page legible while still feeling bold.

## Why this mix

- `Hybrid Vigor` gives the page a high-chroma, internet-native backbone.
- `Amanda Yeh Piano Scrollbar Piano` adds tempo and a tactile color sweep.
- `Developments` keeps the composition sharp, typographic, and culture-aware.

## What I intentionally avoided

- Reusing the exact reference stack from `blind-build-01`.
- Pure SaaS references that would flatten the launch into product-dashboard energy.
- A one-to-one imitation of any single site; this page is a recombination, not a copy.
