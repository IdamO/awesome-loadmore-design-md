# Pics of People Taking Pics Inspired Design System

[DESIGN.md](./DESIGN.md) extracted from the public [Pics of People Taking Pics](https://picsofpeopletakingpics.ju.mp/) website, cross-referenced with [loadmo.re](https://loadmo.re/posts/pics-of-people-taking-pics). This is not the official design system. The goal is to give an AI agent enough grounded design language to recreate the feel without flattening it into generic SaaS UI.
